I’m a student at the University of Pittsburgh majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Studio Arts and related area in Psycholgy.

I chose to major in CS after taking an intoduction class as a requirement for the Psych major. I really enjoyed learning about all the fascinating things you could do with coding. Throughout my life, I have also enjoyed messing around with my computer as a result of testing out many digital drawing programs. As I have taken more advanced CS classes, I have come to enjoy the debugging process and coming up with different solutions.

I have been drawing and interested in art my entire life. I have experience with a plethora of digital drawing software, as I started drawing digitally at a young age. I also dabbled with video editing back then when I made time-lapse videos of my drawing process. I feel that art and design is an essential part of who I am, and plays a role in the way I think about the world.

I’d describe my design style as “things that I like”. This mindset compels me to understand the requests and desires of the client. I'd like to make their ideal design a reality. I've taken a few psychology classes for my related area in psycholgy to understand what stands out to the human brain the most.
